## Data Science Salary Trends 2023

Data science roles are becoming more prevalent and essential for businesses worldwide to maximize quality and financial success. With appropriate skills and experience, industries welcome professionals with impressive packages.

### 02\. Data Science Salary by Experience

![data science salary by experience](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/salary-by-experience.png)

-   The average median salary of a data scientist in India is around INR 15,20,000.
-   Data science and analytics professionals’ salary bracket has seen a 60% jump in the last 8 years.
-   The median salary for data science professionals dipped by 10% from 2022 to 2023.

### 03\. Salary by Cities in India

![data science salary by cities](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/salary-by-cities.png)

-   The average data science salary bangalore is highest owing to the fact that Bangalore is the leading tech city of India with the largest number of data science job opportunities and research facilities
-   Salary brackets in Gurgaon and New Delhi are highest compared to other Indian cities. New Delhi and Gurgaon are home to several data science innovations, research, and tech companies
-   NCR has seen a 17% dip in salary compared to last year 
-   Owing to high living costs, Mumbai has seen a 4% rise in the salary bracket since 2022

### 04\. Salary by Industries

![data science salary by industry](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/data-science-salary-by-industry.png)

![salary by industry](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/salary-by-industry.png)

-   Data professionals working in the Internet/eCommerce industry earn 1.4 times more than the benchmark salary, followed by the Banking and Finance sector. 
-   The lowest median salary is recorded for travel, energy, and media professionals. 
-   This variation is largely due to the huge content creation in the internet domain that has resulted in more user data to analyze. 
-   Data professionals in the media domain can increase their salary by 6 times with over a decade of experience.

### 05\. Salary by Gender

-   The pay gap issue remains unsolved. Reports show that, globally, women earn $0.83 for every $1 men make, which is 20% less than men. 
-   Of all G20 countries, India is at 48.1% regarding gender pay differences.

### 06\. Salary by Job roles

![data science salary by roles](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/data-science-salary-by-roles.png)

-   Data Architect is one of the highest paying job roles. This is because a Data Architect directly works with stakeholders to understand the purpose of data analysis and how the data will be collected, analyzed, and used for business decisions. 
-   Data Architect role is closely followed by AI engineers who are responsible for various modern-day innovations, especially when the world is embracing AI like never before. 

### 07\. Salary by Tools

![data science salary by tools](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/data-science-salary-by-tools.png)

-   On average, professionals skilled in Kuberflow have the highest salary. 
-   Experience-wise, Kuberflow has the highest salary bracket compared to all other tools. However, for professionals with 6-10 years of experience, MLFlow has a higher salary bracket at 29.6L than Kuberflow at 27.4L.

### 08\. Salary by Company Type

![data science salary by company](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/data-science-salary-by-company.png)

-   Startups have thehighest salary for data scientist in india, irrespective of their experience level. Beginners \[0-3 years\] have a salary of 9.9L, while professionals with 12+ years of experience earn 56.3L. 
-   Startups are closely followed by Domestic Firms with 0-3 years earning 7.6L and 12+ years of experience earning 18.8L. However, domestic firms saw a -18% dip in salary trends since last year.

## Data Science Industry – 2023 and Beyond

A job in data science requires significant effort, a passion for data, and a specific curiosity about problem-solving. The demand for data science workers is rising as more companies and organizations recognize the importance of making data-driven decisions.

According to Glassdoor, data scientist positions are among the high-paying jobs in the U.S., with an average salary of $113k annually. Moreover, there is projected to be a significant increase in data science job openings in the coming years. 

**Key Statistics**

-   According to McKinsey research, businesses that provide all of their employees access to data are **40 times** more likely to claim that analytics positively affects sales.
-   By 2026, the market for AIaaS is anticipated to be worth $43.298 billion, expanding at an astounding CAGR of 48.9%.
-   By 2029, it is anticipated that the industry for big data analytics will be worth more than $650 billion. 
-   Experts forecast that by the end of 2023, GDPR-like rules will apply to 65% of the world’s population.
-   According to MIT Sloan Management Review research, businesses that use data-driven decision-making are **6%** more lucrative and **5%** more productive than their rivals.
-   A Forrester Research analysis claims that data-driven focused marketing can increase marketing ROI by **10% to 20%.**
-   According to a Boston Consulting Group report, businesses that use data-driven personalization see a **6–10%** gain in revenue.
-   The Association of Certified Fraud Examiners survey claims that businesses that use data analytics see a 52% decrease in losses due to fraud and a 50% increase in detection.
-   Companies that use data-driven analytics to optimize their supply chains can reduce overall costs by up to 10%.
-   According to a report by the McKinsey Global Institute, companies that effectively use data analytics are **23 times** more likely to attract customers, **6 times** more likely to keep clients, and **19 times** more likely to be profitable.
