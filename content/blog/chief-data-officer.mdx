Chief Data Officer – New Seat in C-suite

As an analytics professional, it is really heartening to see the birth of terms such as Chief Data Officer and Chief Analytics Officer. I bet you must have never came such terms in your whole lifetime.

You might have heard a few or all of the following:

-   Chief Executive Office (CEO)
-   Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
-   Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
-   Chief People’s Officer (CPO)
-   Chief Information Officer (CIO)
-   Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)
-   Chief Operations Officer (COO), etc

Now with the advent of unlimited data and requirement to analyze most (if not all) of it, such kind of hi-profile personnel are needed to run the show.

Personally, I feel excited and very much enthusiastic about the growth of so called Data-Guru’s who are making their lasting impact in the modern world.

Kudos to all the leaders and future data and analytics professionals
