The growing dependence on data and analytics has completely transformed the paradigm of doing business. If you look at the job market, you would realize it’s turning to be a very competitive world, driven by an obvious shift in how businesses now look out for candidates with certifications and expertise in Data Management, Data Analytics, Deep Learning and other related areas.

According to a leading research company, every Fortune 500 company is either actively looking to hire or already have a data science engineering or Deep Learning team driving their data-based operations. By 2020, at least 50% of these companies would completely automate their data science projects to intelligent virtual assistants and technologies.

So, what does that say about the future in data science and analytics market?

Two things—

_First, the big 500 companies of the world would require talented, data-driven professionals to run their business._

_Second, people are going to lose their job to automation and data science tools. It’s going to be a tough competition between man and technology—90% of the times it would be technology coming on top._

To stay on track with the ongoing paradigm shift, you ought to make your CV look powerful and standout from the heaps of other documents that Hiring Managers in Data Science companies are screening through every day.

Here are seven “powerful” terms in Data Science landscape that makes your CV impressive and outstanding for that coveted job you are applying to.

### **Augmented Intelligence**

Besides Artificial Intelligence (AI), there is another A.I. that trend watchers expect to drive businesses of ‘tomorrow’. It’s Augmented Intelligence. In Online Analytics course, Augmented Intelligence is explained as an extended branch of Artificial Intelligence. Augmented Intelligence is conceptualized as the interaction of machine learning, deep learning, cognitive science and robotics.

### **Blockchain**

Analytics is transforming the currency and fintech market with its distributed ledger protocols. If you are applying for a Predictive Analytics job, make sure you have the basic knowledge about how the Blockchain works. Many experts in the data science have already identified the advantages of merging the Blockchain into predictive analytics to disrupt the pricing and pay-per-use models in the coming years.

### **Native Cloud Application**

Native Cloud Application or NPA as it is called in the industry is built on top of traditional Cloud Computing architecture. With online analytics, NPA is a noticeable skill that could push your DevOps career into the next realm of success. For companies that offer DevOps as a service to cloud companies, finding this skill in your CV is a definite eye catcher.

### **Quantum Computing**

Not long ago, experts believed that quantum computing is still a galaxy away from making their presence felt in the commercial system. However, with recent advancements made by IBM through it Quantum Volume Score, things have changed drastically. It is squeezing in more raw data to deliver powerful real-time analytics with high speed and performance. As the race to master Quantum Computing services catches up with the job market, it’s your chance to dive into it now.

### **Advanced Python Topics**

Ahh, now we are talking about the substance that paddles the whole flywheel of innovation in Data Science and Analytics industry. Advanced Python programming skills could virtually place you in the league of top data analysts and engineers working with top data aggregation companies.

Beginners in Python course can focus on building their expertise in topics like Finite State Machines, matplotlib, NumPy, Numerical Computations, Linear Combination, Computer Linguistics, Text Classification and sys module.

### **Robotic Process Automation**

Marketing, Sales, Services, Finance and HR are readily adopting the latest technology trend in technology with successful results. Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is the software application of AI, Machine Learning and Robotic capabilities that are designed to automate highly repetitive, high volume tasks. Considered as the Digital Twin of Internet of Things, RPA is the top component of all major IT operations. Professionals in Business Intelligence, Customer Service and Digital Transformation projects can certainly benefit the most from learning about RPA from leading Online Analytics course in India.

### R

In CV, this single alphabet can make a difference to your salary negotiations. While most people would argue its validation in the modern landscape, R Programming skills are definitely the most sought after in data science projects.

With the basic understanding of these terms under your belt, you can certainly impress the interview panel.
