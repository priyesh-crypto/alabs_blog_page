**What is Data Analytics?**

Data analytics, or in some cases within the industry – data analysis, means the compelling examination of any data set to draw comprehensible conclusions about the information they have within.

Any data analytics process would involve these steps—

–          Data collection/ mining

–          Data storage

–          Data inspection

–          Data cleansing

–          Data structuring

–          Data transformation

–          Data restructuring/ remodeling

–          Finding ‘dark’ data

The final objective of the data analytics is to discover the useful information various data sets carry for a wide range of applications, including for businesses, education and training, science and economy sectors.

**What Do You Exactly Mean By Data Requirements?**

The first question professionals applying for Data Analytics Courses Online ask—

_“What kind of data would I be dealing with?”_

Data requirements, if simply put, pertains to the information that makes up for the input. It could be structured or unstructured, depending on the source. The data requirements are often decided by the analysis team or the customers who are going to benefit from the data analytics.

During the Data Analytics Courses Online, students would learn the informative quotient of statistical applications used to extract and classify data into categories – Numerical and Text linked to number, object, etc.
