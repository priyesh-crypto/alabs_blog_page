Artificial Intelligence (AI)  and its advanced cousin Machine Learning (ML) are skills that one should definitely earn if one is aiming for a career which not only offers high salary packages but also great job security. At a time when disruptive technologies are having an impact on industries and economies all over the world, demand for AI and ML experts are bound to increase. ## **How AI is vital for organizations?**

Digital transformation has already swept through the globe and global industries are undergoing a sea of changes. There is an urgent need for automation and digitization as the fourth industrial revolution is already here. In such a scenario no business organization can afford to remain unchanged and should not only try to keep up with quickly advancing technology but also try to stay ahead of it in order to remain competitive!

AI is one disruptive technology that has received much assistance lately due to an increased emphasis on automation. Businesses need automation for greater efficiency and for streamlining business operations so that precious time is saved and along with it precious resources. Moreover, if AI is implemented across all business operations then the workforce can focus on more creative tasks and rely on AI for all the mundane and time consuming tasks. On the top of that AI has several other advantages for any business organization:

-   Greater customer intimacy thereby increasing consumer demand
-   Automating processes which in turn can help in multiplying productivity gains!
-   A massive increase in competitive advantage
-   Improved data analytics

Thus, any organization small or big must adopt AI today.

## **Machine Learning**

Machine Learning is the ability of machines to learn and perform through data without the need for explicitly programming. Machine Learning algorithms help computer systems to learn from data and improve on its own thereby reaching great potential. ML is being applied to a variety of fields today and has amazing advantages. A few examples of ML use are:

-   Speech and text recognition
-   Medical diagnosis
-   Virtual assistants
-   Chatbots
-   Fraud detection systems
-   Recommendation engines

Moreover, ML is not only being utilized by private enterprises but also by governmental agencies and academic institutions. For instance, the government of India is using ML for creating a system capable of flagging tax-evaders which is named Project Insight. Moreover, medical research facilities are utilizing ML for diagnosis of rare diseases like cancer and have been largely successful so far. Experts also suggest that ML is going to replace humans in multiple fields in the near future due to ML’s better accuracy in a range of activities.
