Carrying the tag of being the IT hub in India has its own charm and excitement. That’s the exact reason why Bangalore enjoys higher attention from multinational companies looking to hire Data scientists, IT specialists, Data analysts and AI researchers. Analytics courses in Bangalore have opened new avenues for professionals, engineering students and other graduates to build their career in intelligent data collection and analysis.

This article throws a research-based spotlight on how multinational companies actually look at analytics training in Bangalore and other parts of the country.

### Data Literacy is Highest in Bangalore

According to a speculation on a social media platform, Bangalore employees the highest number of CIOs, CTOs, CPOs, data scientists and other Analytics-specific jobs. Closely following the city are Chennai, Mumbai, Pune and Delhi-NCR. Data literacy among Bangalore IT/ ITES professionals is often speculated to be the highest compared to those employed in the other Indian cities, including Mumbai and Chennai. With a sharp rise in the number of job openings for AI and machine learning related experts, Bangalore analytics training could solve a large gap in the industry.

### Practical and Theory Research Labs Growing To Meet the Industry Requirements

According to a forecast by a leading consultancy firm, US and Western European countries are falling short of more than 2 million managers and analysts. A large part of the gap is a factor that Indian analytics training industry is not providing enough professionals. Analytics training institutes in Bangalore are gearing up to solve this local as well as global employment issues.

Big Data analytics courses taught in the premier Business schools, including government-funded ones, can’t cater to the industry demands alone. The biggest challenge lies in the lack of certified analytics experts teaching at business schools. Analytics training courses are filling the gap to an extent by helping companies recruit from their institutions.

### Training Creates Business Value; Companies Willing to Partner with Analytics Training Institutes

With planned collaboration in Applied AI and machine learning with Bangalore analytics training institutes, multi-national companies are looking to solve their employment gaps. Did you know that MNCs shell out nearly 40 percent of their annual training budget on equipping their own employees, and not fresher graduates and students?

A solid foundation in Bangalore analytics training partnership could reduce the cost of training these employees significantly. Also, the companies can create higher business value at a sustainable rate for projects that largely include IT analysts, managers and CTOs.

### Global Companies Increase Investments in and around Bangalore

IBM Bangalore in India is one of the highest employing firms in India, largely recruiting IT specialists, Technology officers and data analysts. Last decade saw IBM recruiting 800% higher number of Indian employees compared to the preceding decade. Similar numbers floor out for SAP, Oracle and HP, taken together. Indian and multinational companies are further investing in highly specialized data-related projects like Big Data appliances, IoT, Hybrid Cloud infrastructure, Marketing automation, Sales intelligence, AI and machine storytelling. All these harbor pool of expertise solutions in IT and related services that only premier talent from Data [analytics training Bangalore](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/) could take on.

## The Other Side of the Growth: Challenges in Bangalore Analytics Training Courses

What can analytics training and technology institutes do more to drive the impressive growth in the market? Most experts from multinational companies would agree at two things and recommend doing it right.

Firstly, involve industry experts and certified analysts to design course curriculum. The analytics education with active industry recommendation could be very helpful to every participant in the industry, including employers.

Secondly, the analytics training courses should access cases studies and competition papers to design their own data sets and build algorithms. There are a certain cost and resource attached to it. The involvement in these cases could enhance the data mining skills to use the various already-available analytics training modules wisely.

## Do all this guarantee Jobs as Desired?

When you are dealing with the analytics industry, listen to what the industry insiders have to say about the roles they are looking to hire. For example, analytics training courses will train you for skills and knowledge, and not merely qualification. Companies prefer skills over qualifications during the interview – your gateway to a career in applied data and analytics fields. A desirous job would require you to refine your analytics skills; that’s where analytics training in Bangalore make the biggest influence on the job market.
