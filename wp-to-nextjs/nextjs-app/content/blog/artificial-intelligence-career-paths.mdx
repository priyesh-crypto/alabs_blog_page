## **What Is AI for Beginners?**

From a beginner’s perspective, Artificial Intelligence is the ability of a system to replicate the capabilities of human intelligence. It is when machines are simulated to undergo cognitive behaviors like learning and decision making by training the systems. It is a recurring process of data input, statistical analyses, and pattern formation to understand the behavior and generate results. We use AI in the forms of Google search algorithms, recommendation systems, autonomous weapons, and even self-driven automobiles. 

![](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/blogs-banner-6a.jpg)

> **AI-based systems are defined by two approaches:** _**Reactive Machines**_ **and** _**Limited memory AI**_

Artificial Intelligence is a revolutionary technology that utilizes the machine learning reactive models and the limited memory models based on AI. 

The **_reactive machine_** simply takes up the input and produces some output without performing any learning. These machines belong to the first stage of AI systems. Examples of such systems are static machine learning models. 

The **_limited memory models_** enforce the storage of previous data and learning capabilities which help in generating better predictions for the future. The limited memory model follows two approaches. 

1.  A team continuously trains the model with new data.
2.  Training and renewing the models automatically in the AI environment. 

Further, there are types of AI like [Theory of Mind](https://medium.com/appengine-ai/type-3-of-functional-ai-theory-of-mind-ai-33205c8b640e) and [Self Aware AI](https://www.expresscomputer.in/artificial-intelligence-ai/what-if-ai-becomes-self-aware/81828/) whose mysteries are still unsolved. These approaches of AI are expected to interact with human emotions and intervene with their thoughts.

Before we start discussing on the subject “Is artificial intelligence a good career?”, let’s look at the types of AI we generally deal with. AI generally falls under two broad categories, namely Narrow AI and Artificial General Intelligence.

### Types of AI

We should first explore AI types to understand career opportunities in artificial intelligence. All the real, and hypothetical AI problems and solutions, basically fall into three categories:

-   **_Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI)_**

-   **_Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)_**

-   **_Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI)_**

#### **Artificial Narrow Intelligence/ Narrow AI**

Narrow AI also termed as weak AI, is a target-oriented AI approach that is designed to carry out singular tasks like internet searches, biometric recognition, and self-driven cars. These systems are trained to perform dedicated tasks with human-like intelligence. They are programmed with narrow constraints thus, the name Weak AI.

Narrow AI can work on either a reactive model or a limited memory model depending upon the volume of data and the required output. These systems are designed to interact with their users in a customized and natural manner. Narrow AI has kept on developing using deep learning, machine learning, and Natural language processing (NLP) algorithms. Most of the problems fall under the Narrow AI category, opening endless career opportunities in artificial intelligence.

Narrow AI examples:

-   Voice assistants like Siri, Alexa, Cortana
-   Watson by IBM
-   Email spam filters
-   Social media content monitoring
-   Personalized recommendation systems
-   Image/ Speech/ Face recognition
-   Chatbots

#### **Artificial General Intelligence/ Deep AI /Strong AI**

> Artificial General Intelligence also termed _**Deep AI**_ is an approach wherein the systems can replicate human intelligence by undergoing self-learning capabilities.

Strong AI systems can think, analyze and react to problems like a human does without human intervention. In order to succeed in implementing Deep AI systems, the systems must be fed with a full set of cognitive capabilities to make conscious machines. Researchers and scientists are trying hard to get into the Deep AI universe such that it should have only a positive impact on the universe.

AGI may utilize the theory of mind approach of AI that enables interaction and bifurcation of emotions, beliefs, and cognitive processes of various intellectual beings. The fictional I-Robots and The Terminator portrayed in the sci-fi stories can be tagged as an implementation of Strong AI systems. 

#### **Artificial Super Intelligence**

Artificial Super Intelligence is the theoretical AI algorithm that may completely understand and replicate human behavior and intelligence. These may be self-aware and self-sufficient that can surpass human capabilities. The impact of the ASI may have multiple consequences in both positive and negative quadrants making its implementation under strict investigation.

## **Pre-requisites Skills for an AI job**

Having a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree or any certification course in the IT and computer science, statistics, and data science fields can help to build a stronger cognitive foundation for the course. Although there are some specialized technical and nontechnical skills that can be essential to understand the AI algorithms and platforms.

![skills for AI job](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/skills-for-AI-job.png)

### Technical

-   _**Programming languages**_– C++, R, Java, Lisp, Python, and Prolog are the programming languages that are used in learning AI fundamentals. Knowledge of classes and data structures helps in understanding the algorithms with ease. Python and R have gained more popularity due to their libraries, clear syntax, simplicity, and versatility. 
-   _**Machine learning**_: Machine learning is a supervised learning algorithm that utilizes large datasets to enable decision-making and predictions from the patterns. ML is basically implemented using neural networks, which cluster the data based on pattern, image, and speech recognition.
-   _**Natural Language Processing**_: A combination of computer science and linguistics to process and understand human languages. AI professionals will have to work on language, video, and audio processing including semantic and sentiment analysis.
-   _**Data Science and Analytics**_: A repetitive process wherein the data is collected, manipulated, and drilled upon to extract useful insights from it. The analytics help in finding patterns necessary for supervised learning. AI professionals have to excel in the analytical approach toward data and numbers. 
-   _**Database Management systems**_: Apache Cassandra, PostgreSQL, Couchbase, MLDB, and Microsoft SQL Server are the most used databases in machine learning and AI models.
-   _**Applied mathematics and calculus**_: AI works on conventional techniques like linear regression, clustering, Bayesian modeling, random forest analysis, and logistic regression. AI professionals need to learn the procedures involved in the aforesaid techniques to use them in future AI algorithms.

### Non-technical:

-   _**Communication and Collaboration skills**_: The task of AI professionals is to portray the prospects of the fields to the stakeholders involved. The skilled professional will have to clearly identify the findings and convey the results to ensure success. In addition, Strong visualization skills may help in delivering uncovered insights and conveying them in alluring storytelling ways, to attain growth and outcomes.

## **How to Switch Career to Artificial Intelligence**

Before switching a career to Artificial Intelligence, you have to understand that it is a constantly evolving domain. You have to have that constant thirst for knowledge. The knowledge that you gain today might not be relevant 3-5 yrs down the line. So let’s break up the path for you into two streams, one for the people new to IT and the other for people with IT experience but in non-AI technologies.

### **Non-IT background**

If you are coming in from a non-IT background, you may be coming in with a wealth of domain knowledge that AI can use to improve the system. However, you could also use your domain knowledge and new skills to give yourself the edge. Let’s list down the skills you should gain before or while on the switch over to AI.

-   Firstly, gain enough skill in Python, the easiest programming language. Get the programming DNA right. You can start with simple coding exercises and scale up quickly in Python. To learn to program, you need to know a few concepts in computer science. For example, you could start writing algorithms for smaller programs, giving you a general understanding of ML algorithms.
-   Learn about database management systems.
-   For Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Neural Networks, an understanding of mathematics are important. So pick up the required knowledge in mathematics. If you don’t plan to get your hands dirty with ML code, you can skip this one.
-   Learn Probability, Basic Statistics, and Random Variables.
-   Learn about Machine Learning Algorithms. What is the objective of Machine Learning? How is Machine Learning achieved? What are the packages and tools that assist in programming ML/Deep Learning/Natural Language Processing?

Each of the above requirements might require you to gain further related knowledge.

### **IT background**

It would be safe to assume that you have some experience with programming languages and database management systems from an IT background.

-   Gain programming experience in Python or R.
-   Use Data Visualization packages in Python or R to draw meaningful visualizations. Then, learn how to weave your story around the visualizations.
-   Gain knowledge in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Neural Networks.
-   Brush up on Statistics-Probability, Combinatorics, Bayesian Statistics, and Descriptive Statistics.
-   Try Machine Learning in Python or R. Learn about pandas, Scikit Learn, Seaborn, and the packages available in Python for data science and machine learning algorithms.
