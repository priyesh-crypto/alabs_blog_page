## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI and machine learning-powered model developed from OpenAI, a leading artificial intelligence research devwp. It uses natural language processing (NLP) and deep learning to generate human-like responses to conversations in an intelligent manner.

> ChatGPT has been trained on millions of conversation examples worldwide, allowing it to understand how humans communicate and respond accordingly.

The popularity has skyrocketed, especially for chatbots and virtual assistants. Since ChatGPT is programmed to comprehend and interpret human language, it may provide responses virtually identical to those of a human being.

![chatgpt features](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-29-at-5.11.11-PM.png)

### The Technology Behind ChatGPT

Open AI ChatGPT is a natural language understanding (NLU) system based on the **Transformer architecture**. It was trained on a large amount of text data using unsupervised learning. This means it did not get explicit instructions about what to learn but instead discovered patterns and relationships independently.

To produce an appropriate response, it uses the knowledge to produce an understandable response through a cohesive step-by-step process. Open AI ChatGPT’s capacity to produce natural and human-like language is one of its advantages. It enables the model to comprehend the context and provide answers suitable for the particular circumstance.

Note: ChatGPT’s [data cut-off is at 2021](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/6827058-why-doesn-t-chatgpt-know-about-x). This can be an explanation to why ChatGPT may not know about recent trends or news.

![chatgpt data](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-29-at-5.18.45-PM.png)

Even though the cut-off date is 2021, ChatGPT has the capability to come up with accurate responses based on its training data. Its living proof is a recent story on how ChatGPT aided in [saving a dog’s life by making a correct diagnosis](https://twitter.com/peakcooper/status/1639716833895886850?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw%7Ctwcamp%5Etweetembed%7Ctwterm%5E1639716833895886850%7Ctwgr%5Efbf5d5d9eb00c364dec2e00263cda7e9a9b96829%7Ctwcon%5Es1_&ref_url=https%3A%2F%2Feconomictimes.indiatimes.com%2Fnews%2Fnew-updates%2Fchatgpt-saves-dogs-life-gives-correct-diagnosis-that-even-vet-could-not-identify%2Farticleshow%2F99053583.cms) of the blood test results.

## Chatbots vs. AI Writer

**Chatbots**

Chatbots are different from AI writing tools. A chatbot is designed to mimic human-user conversations through text or voice exchanges. It works to resolve user inquiries quickly. However, a chatbot will not be able to handle complicated queries because a chatbot will not have the capability to ‘formulate an answer’ on its own. This is because a chatbot is trained with data pertaining to a particular brand and general user queries. 

For instance, the chat help we see on every website can capture first-level user data, help with help docs about the brand, and even show you _relevant pages_ for your query. But when it comes to handling complex queries about the product, it will either falter and repeat the same answer or prompt back with ‘raising a ticket to connect you with one of our representatives’. Here’s how –

The Amazon Chatbot will continue showing the same options. This chatbot is trained to answer queries about orders, delivery, product info, account details, etc. Anything more complicated than this is beyond its capabilities.

[https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/chatbot-conversation.mp4](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/chatbot-conversation.mp4)

To escalate or resolve your issue, you must manually type in that you need more support. The chatbot will catch your phrase and automatically show you options to connect with an agent.  It will then connect you to one of the representatives who can help resolve the issue; like this:

![chatbot amazon](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Screenshot-2023-03-30-at-10.56.21-AM.png)

Chatbots do not make up content. They are built to handle user queries and resolve them quickly. A chatbot is trained to escalate the issue if it has no answer.

**AI Writers**

AI Writing tools are different. They are more intelligent and are capable of writing long-form articles. AI Writers are trained in a vast volume of text data. This makes them capable of producing human-like content on a given subject or keyword.

For instance, look at [Writesonic](https://writesonic.com/), an AI-powered writing assistant that can easily generate short-form and long-form content.

[https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/AI-writer-chatsonic.mp4](https://www.analytixlabs.co.in/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/AI-writer-chatsonic.mp4)

Tools like Writesonic are trained on advanced machine learning algorithms to generate or produce human-friendly content. Tools like these are highly intelligent and capable of _learning_ independently and creating content on any subject matter. It gets smarter by the day, analyzing your writing patterns and suggestions and improving its content based on the learning.

#### How do AI Chatbots Work?

AI chatbots combine machine learning techniques and NLP to replicate human-like conversations with consumers. The chatbot uses NLP to decipher the user’s purpose and interpret the input. After then, based on the input it received and its prior contacts with the user, it applies machine learning algorithms to choose the best response.

Chatbots can be pre-programmed with predefined answers for frequent user requests or trained on massive datasets of human talks. They can be connected with websites or chat services to offer real-time support and boost user engagement. Chatbots are growing more complex as technology develops, with the capacity to recognize context, mood, and emotional states.

## How to Choose the Right Chatbot?

ChatGPT is the most robust open-source conversational AI platform available, providing powerful features and capabilities for natural language understanding and text generation. However, it ultimately depends on your needs and the complexity of the conversations you plan to have.

For instance, if you want to create basic chatbots with predefined responses, you can use platforms like Dialogflow, which feature graphical user interfaces that make it easy to create simple bots without any coding knowledge required. The platforms provide more advanced features like microservices integration and NLP capabilities for more sophisticated conversations.

Jasper AI might be a better fit if you need more advanced NLP and text generation capabilities. It is a powerful tool to generate text in response to given prompts and has been used to create realistic content. However, its open-source nature requires some technical knowledge to use correctly.

## FAQs

### 1\. Are there any other websites like Chat GPT?

Although several ChatGPT alternatives are available in the market, Jasper, Chatsonic, and Microsoft Bing are the leading ones. These AI tools are powered by upgraded technology and are backed by extended features with internet accessibility.

### 2\. Which AI is better than ChatGPT?

According to OpenAI, GPT-4 is more capable and better than ChatGPT. Based on an upgraded GPT version, GPT-4 is a multimodal large language model. It can effectively respond to both texts and messages. 

GPT-4 is also claimed to be 60% less likely to make stuff up. And in comparison to GPT – 3.5, it is 82% less likely to respond to requests for content that OpenAI does not allow.

### 3\. Is there a free version of ChatGPT?

Yes, the basic version of ChatGPT can be accessed for free. Since it is available via a webpage, there is no need to download it. The web page is quite simple and includes an area for the results. It also offers a text box at the bottom of the page to ensure seamless user interrogation.

### 4\. What is Google’s equivalent to ChatGPT?

Google BARD is the newly released Google equivalent to ChatGPT. This experimental conversational AI service is powered by Google’s lightweight model version of LaMDA. It brings in information directly from the web and provides the latest responses. However, it is currently available to a few trusted testers, as the company plans to add more advanced AI features to Google search.

### 5\. Which AI is better than ChatGPT?

Chinchilla AI is considerably better than ChatGPT and is called the “GPT Killer.” It is an AI language model created by DeepMind, a subdivision of Google. However, it is nothing similar to Google BARD.

The company claims Chinchilla outperforms its competing LLMs and even predominant models like GPT-3. 

However, it is not yet available for public use, so users looking forward to accessing this alternative will have to wait until the release.

### 6\. Who is the competitor of GPT?

Although GPT is the internet’s favorite, numerous similar platforms are determined to replace GPT with its exceptional features. Some are OPT, BLOOM, Google LAMDA, PALM, and Character AI.

### 7\. Is Google Meena available?

Google Meena is not yet available to access for the end users. The company says the software is still under evaluation for associated risks and benefits of externalizing the model checkpoints. However, it is expected to be released soon to enhance the productivity of advanced research in the field.
