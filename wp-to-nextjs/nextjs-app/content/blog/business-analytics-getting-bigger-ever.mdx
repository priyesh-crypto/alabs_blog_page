## _What is business analysis and the work of business analysts?_

We have time and again proven that age old adage of “change is the only constant factor”; is the only truth. And our businesses are no different; every fiscal year brings new challenges and we have to constantly keep ourselves in sync with the changing market trends so that the objectives of the organisation are maintained and we can have profitable turn over at the end of the year.

This is where the business analysts come into play; they have an integral role in the functioning of any organisation.  They unearth the need for change and implement the solutions that will help in giving the maximum return on investment to the stakeholders. Business analysts work through the entire hierarchy of an organisation and take a stock of past values and implement current technologies to take the establishment forward in the future.

## _Difference between business analyst and business intelligence._

A word that we come across often is data analyst, often in connection with business analyst or even in standalone context. Many people do not understand the fine distinction between the two and is used ambiguously, one for the other. But more often than not, it deals with the statistical analysis of data; hence, the distinction gets blurred within the corporate setting.

Now, to help you get clearer perspective, we shall discuss another term, business intelligence. BI is a conglomeration of technologies, applications for integration, analysis and presentation of business information. It helps to improve the effectiveness, efficiency and productivity of the organisation. which will help them in dodging the competition and keeping ahead in the race.
