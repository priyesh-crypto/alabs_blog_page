## **Detecting Plagiarism – Deployment of Advanced Algorithms**

There are many methodologies deployed to detect plagiarism in the content, one of which is the longest common subsequence. It could be explained as a sequence of words from left to right while comparing with other text. It usually works in comparison to a text with another one from the web. It detects the common word recursively. It is the most dynamic method; along with that, there’s another method known as containment. A corpus is formed in comparing the provided text with other copies available over the web. However, there are other methods as well, like converting categorical to numerical data to find similarities.
